About LocalWiki

LocalWiki is a project of Wiki Spot, a 501(c)(3) nonprofit community improvement organization based in San Francisco, California.

The LocalWiki project is being led primarily by Philip Neustrom and Mike Ivanov, the co-founders of the Davis Wiki. There's a lot more people involved — we'll add their names here shortly!

We're best known for Davis Wiki, which Harvard's Nieman Journalism Lab recently called "The world's best local wiki".
